WHAT IS HACKATHON ?


Key purposes of a hackathon are Innovation, Problem Solving, Learning and Skill Development, Networking, Team Building, Prototype Development, Competition and Community Building.
TCE Hacks is the annual hackathon organized by TCE CSE Association, Thiagarajar College Of Engineering, Madurai. It is an in-person 24-hr event for innovatively fostering real-world problem-solving skills.


  • Date: 18th September to 19th September 2023
  • Team Size: 2 to 4 members
  • Free registrations
  • Participation fee of Rs. 200 to be paid after getting shortlisted
  • Cash prizes will be announced soon!
